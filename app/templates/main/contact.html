Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a training module?

Training modules vary in length from 2-40 hours depending on the complexity and certification level. Each module includes interactive content, hands-on exercises, and assessments.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes! We provide certificates of completion for all training modules. For certification preparation courses, we also offer exam vouchers and additional study materials.

Is there technical support available?

Absolutely! Our technical support team is available 24/7 to help with any issues or questions you may have during your training.

Can I access training materials offline?

Yes, many of our training materials can be downloaded for offline access. This includes PDFs, videos, and interactive exercises.
